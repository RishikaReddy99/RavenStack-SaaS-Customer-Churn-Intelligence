🌐 RavenStack SaaS Customer Churn Intelligence
Business Analysis Case Study

Identifying Customer Retention Risks, Revenue Leakage & Growth Opportunities

🛠 Tools Used

Excel | MySQL | Power BI | GitHub

📊 Dataset Overview
Domain: SaaS / B2B Customer Analytics
Records: ~500 Customer Accounts
Tables: 5 Relational Tables
Period: Jan 2023 – Dec 2024
Source: Kaggle

📌 Executive Summary

RavenStack is a B2B SaaS company preparing for public launch. This analysis was conducted to understand customer churn behavior, revenue leakage, and retention risks before scaling operations.

The study combines Excel (EDA), SQL (business analysis), and Power BI (executive reporting) to identify key churn drivers.

🔑 Key Insights:
DevTools customers show the highest churn volume
Paid acquisition channels underperform organic channels
Most customers churn after 90+ days of usage
Pro plan generates highest revenue loss
Feature gaps are the leading cause of refunds

🎯 Business Problem

RavenStack lacked visibility into:

Why customers were churning
Which segments were at highest risk
When churn was happening in the lifecycle
Which actions could reduce revenue loss

🧠 Business Objectives
Identify high-risk customer segments
Measure churn impact on revenue
Understand churn timing in lifecycle
Analyze acquisition channel performance
Recommend retention strategies

👥 Stakeholder Analysis
CEO / Leadership: churn & revenue visibility
Product Team: feature gaps & product improvements
Marketing Team: acquisition channel quality
Sales Team: high-value customer targeting
Support Team: churn-linked issues

💡 Business Insights
1. Long-term churn is the real issue
Customers leave after 90+ days → retention gap
2. DevTools is a high-risk segment
Largest contributor to churn volume
3. Paid acquisition underperforms
Organic channels generate better retention
4. Pro plan is revenue critical
Small churn = high financial impact
5. Product gaps drive churn
Not pricing → missing features

🧾 Conclusion
This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Analyst workflow:
Data → Analysis → Insight → Business Decision
It transforms raw SaaS data into actionable retention strategies that can directly reduce churn and protect revenue.
